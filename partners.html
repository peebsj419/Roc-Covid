TOGETHER LET'S ROC COVID-19

Help us keep tabs on COVID-19 in the Greater Rochester region. The better we track it, the faster we can all get back to our lives.

 Start Daily Health Screening Why should I participate?

You can help stop the spread of Covid-19

Sharing how you feel will help scientists and public health experts find new hotspots and identify populations that could be at risk. The data can also reveal which health measures are having the fastest impact so we can apply those learnings in other areas.

How it works:

Answer a few basic questions about yourself. Next, tell us how you’re feeling. It takes just a few seconds.


You’ll receive daily email reminders to take the survey. It’s important to take the survey even on days you feel well. A random sample of participants, both with and without symptoms, will be offered COVID-19 testing to further inform our scientists.

Why should I participate?

We're all looking for something we can do to stop the spread of COVID-19 and help our community get back on its feet. Sharing your symptoms (or lack of) will help scientists and public health experts identify hotspots that could be at risk.

How often should I complete the survey?

Daily. Takes just a few seconds.

How old do I need to be in order to participate?

You need to be at least 18 years old.

How will my data be used?

Healthcare and community leaders will use the data to understand:

  • How the virus may be spreading.

  • Identify areas that may be at risk.

  • Determine how our efforts are working to slow the spread.

Community and healthcare leaders will use this information to decide where to focus testing and whether they should tighten or loosen restrictions, such as stay-at-home and social distancing.

What if I'm healthy and I don't have any symptoms?

Data on healthy people is important, too. It helps us understand what areas are not seeing an increase in COVID-related symptoms.
